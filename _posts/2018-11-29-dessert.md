The event was a journey throughout the different regions of Italy and for a taste of our local dessert traditions, from Venetian TiramisÃ¹ to Sicilian Cannoli, passing through Tuscan Cantucci, Neapolitan Sfogliatelle and many many more.

From sfogliatelle to tiramisÃ¹, from cannoli to gianduiotti, there were plenty of desserts to satisfy everyone's taste!
