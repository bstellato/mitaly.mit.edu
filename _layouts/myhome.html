Welcome to MITaly!

MITaly is the Italian association at MIT. We strive to facilitate a well-connected network of students, faculty, and affiliates who are linked by a shared experience of both MIT and Italian culture.

If you’re in the Boston area, come to our events, and if you’re abroad, stay in touch!
